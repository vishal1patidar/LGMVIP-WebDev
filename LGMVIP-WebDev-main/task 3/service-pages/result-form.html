VIT Bhopal University (also known as VIT Bhopal or VIT University Bhopal) is one of the best private universities located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. It was founded in 2017 as one of the four campuses of Vellore Institute of Technology by Vellore Group of Institutions. The University adopts an inclusive teaching methodology that redefines the process of education and learning. VIT - Bhopal has been accredited with ‘A’ Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

The University has made its place in the Limca Book of Records for placing the highest number of students in the IT sector for seven consecutive years.
