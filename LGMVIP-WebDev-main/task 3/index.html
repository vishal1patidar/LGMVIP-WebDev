About Us

VIT Bhopal University is situated on 150 acres of sprawling lush green Campus at Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. It was established in the year 2017 and led by founder and Chancellor Dr. G Vishwanathan. VIT Bhopal is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC). Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) has four campuses namely –
1- VIT Bhopal
2- VIT Chennai
3- VIT Amaravati
4- VIT Vellore

Our Association

The VIT legacy, which started from Vellore Engineering College 35 years ago to four Universities in India, today, is a result of constant efforts to impart high quality of education. The Govt. of India has recognized VIT as No. 1 Private Institution for Innovation, ARIIA 2019, and the recent recognition, as an Institution of Eminence, has paved a way to focus more on research with international collaborations to move up in the global ranking and contribute in the capacity building of our nation.

MY favourite Faculties

Harihara sir

Dr. Harihara Padhy heads the School of Advanced Sciences and Languages (SASL). He received Ph.D. in Material Science and Engineering from the National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan. In order to gather extensive research experience he served/worked as postdoctoral fellow at National University of Singapore (NUS), King Abdullah University of Science and Technology, Saudi Arabia, National Chiao-Tung University and National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan. His long association with research intensive institute resulted in his serious commitment to teaching and research. He has published high impact 25 research articles in peer-reviewed journals. His articles are well-received in the scientific communities, reflected through H-index of 12 and over 550 citations.

Manas sir

Dr. Mishra has served in different academic and administrative roles at various academic institutes for more than 21 years. Most recently, he served GLA University, Mathura, (2011-2019) for close to 8 years, where apart from his teaching assignments, he was the Deputy Controller of Examinations, Professor-in-charge IT Infrastructure, and Professor-in-charge Purchases; etc. Dr. Mishra holds a PhD. in Computer Science and Engineering and M. Tech. in Software Engineering from MNNIT Allahabad, and a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Shivaji University, Kolhapur.

Shriram sir

Dr. Shriram Raghunathan is the Programme Chair of Gaming Technology in the School of Computing Science & Engineering, VIT Bhopal University, India. He works on Natural Language Processing, Instructional Design and Gaming. He completed his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering in 2008 and did his Masters in Instructional Design (online) from Open University of Malaysia. His significant achievements include setting up India’s first Centre of Excellence in Pervasive Computing in 2007 worth Rs. 5 crore and completion of 4 funded projects (mobile Tamil interfaces, mobile keypad standardization and plagiarism checking) in Tamil Computing since 2004.He has published widely in domains of gaming, cloud computing, natural language processing and instructional design and guided 4 Doctoral students for their PhD since 2014

Sountharranjan sir

Dr. S. Sountharrajan has completed his Ph.D. from Anna University in Information and Communication Engineering and is currently working as a Senior Assistant Professor in the School of Computing Science and Engineering (SCSE) at VIT Bhopal University. He is Program Chair & division head of B.Tech CSE Specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He was sanctioned an amount of Rs.38, 00,000 from the funding agencies like DST, AICTE and ICMR.He has also published papers in 8 International / 6 National Conferences. He was published 18 International journals including 12 Scopus index 3 SCI. He has been certified from reputed companies of India like the IBM, DELL EMC Corporation.

VIT Bhopal University (also known as VIT Bhopal or VIT University Bhopal) is one of the best private universities located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. It was founded in 2017 as one of the four campuses of Vellore Institute of Technology by Vellore Group of Institutions. The University adopts an inclusive teaching methodology that redefines the process of education and learning. VIT - Bhopal has been accredited with ‘A’ Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

The University has made its place in the Limca Book of Records for placing the highest number of students in the IT sector for seven consecutive years.
